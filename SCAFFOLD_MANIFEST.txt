MODELS & RELATIONSHIPS
======================

Contact
  - hasMany: leads, tasks, communications
  - Methods: search(), byTag()

Lead
  - belongsTo: contact, pipeline
  - hasMany: tasks
  - Methods: advance(), markAsLost(), markAsClosed()
  - Statuses: new, contacted, qualified, proposal, negotiation, closed, lost

Pipeline
  - hasMany: leads
  - Methods: getStats() → returns breakdown by status

Task
  - belongsTo: contact, lead
  - Statuses: pending, in_progress, completed, cancelled
  - Priorities: low, medium, high, urgent
  - Methods: complete(), isOverdue(), isDueToday()

Communication
  - belongsTo: contact, lead
  - Types: email, sms, call, note
  - Statuses: sent, pending, failed, delivered, opened, clicked
  - Methods: markDelivered(), markOpened(), markClicked()

Property
  - hasMany: interestedLeads (via lead.property_interest)
